LTV Formulas

  • LTV = Average Purchase Value × Purchase Frequency × Customer Lifespan
  • Profit-Adjusted LTV = LTV × Profit Margin %
  • LTV:CAC Ratio = Profit-Adjusted LTV ÷ CAC
  • Target LTV:CAC ≥ 3:1 for a healthy, scalable business

Frequently Asked Questions

What is a good LTV:CAC ratio?
A LTV:CAC ratio of 3:1 is the benchmark for a healthy business. Under 1:1 means you spend more to acquire customers than they are worth. Above 5:1 may mean you are underinvesting in growth. Many SaaS companies target 3–5:1.
How can I increase customer lifetime value?
Strategies include: improving retention with loyalty programs, offering subscription models, cross-selling and upselling, improving customer service, increasing purchase frequency with email marketing, and personalizing the customer experience.
