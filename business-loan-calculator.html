How Loan Payments Are Calculated

  • Monthly payment: M = P × [r(1+r)^n] / [(1+r)^n − 1]
  • Where P = principal, r = monthly rate (annual/12), n = number of months
  • Each payment covers more principal and less interest over time

Frequently Asked Questions

What is a typical interest rate for a business loan?
Business loan rates vary widely: SBA loans typically 5.5–8%, traditional bank loans 4–13%, online lenders 7–99%. Your rate depends on credit score, business age, revenue, and loan type.
What is the maximum SBA loan amount?
SBA 7(a) loans go up to $5 million with terms up to 25 years for real estate and 10 years for equipment. SBA microloans go up to $50,000 for small businesses and startups.
